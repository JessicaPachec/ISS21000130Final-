Here are some organizations and activists who speak out about food insecurity.

There are many organizations who are willing to help those who are in real need for not only food but also support whether it be physical mental or overall just someone to listen and talk to.

This is An Garagiola-Bernier and activists for food insecurity for her college in Minnesota.

An Garagiola-Bernier is a recent graduate from Hamline University in Minnesota. She is a mixed race, disabled women who fought against food insecurity for not only herself but ale her fellow classmates at her University.

She provided about 1,500lbs of food to about 200 students at her university every month. She is a prim example on how starting small and local can make a change for not only those around you but also inspire others to do the same.
