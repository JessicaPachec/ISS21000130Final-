Coming from low income backgrounds and suffering from the inability to purchase a proper and nutritious meal touches the hearts of many organizations and activists who know all about the struggle

Posted by Jessica Pacheco, Alexandra Pace, and Karthik Sriram on April 28, 2023

There are many organizations who are willing to help those who are in real need for not only food but also support whether it be physical mental or overall just someone to listen and talk to.

For mant people it can be a struggle and reach out for help because they have sense of pride that they need to keep.

But many organizations are really just there to help.There is not on single organization that helps out without the intentions of just truly being there for those who need it for example WHEAT is an organization in Arizona that makes it their mission to feed the people of Arizona with the most healthy and nutritious meal that they possibly can.
