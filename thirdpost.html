Who is affected by food insecurity?

Even people who seem normal and look like they got everything together suffer from food insecurity. We might not notice it but it is and issue that is not spoken about often.

Posted by Alexandra Pace on April 28, 2023

As we know food insecuirty affects people's healths in unimaginable ways. But the people affected are closer than we think. Some experts may even say that there are key factors when it comes to people who suffer from food insecuirty such as race, ethnicty, income, employment, and disability.

According to a study done this year 2023 by Healthy People, about 28.6 of low income household sufferend from food insecuirty in comparasion to the nation average of 10.5%.

This study alone proves that there are a lot of people that go un seen when it comes to bot unemployment and food insecurity.

hwo does food insecurity target people who are unemployed?

Unemployment has an impact on houshold food insecurity because of their inability to be able to provide for basic needs. Low-income households that experience high unemployment are unable to pay bills, make hous payments, and much less pay for good and nutritious food.

Diabled Elders and their struggle.

Many of those who suffer from food insecurity also include disabled adults and elders. Many may have been born that way and no longer have anyone to take care of them which why the end up in awful situations. As a disabled person finding work is harder and their are also health expenses that they have to think about.
